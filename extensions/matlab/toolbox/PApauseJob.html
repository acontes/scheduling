PApauseJob
  PApauseJob pauses the given Scheduler job
 
  Syntax
 
        >> PApauseJob(jobid);
 
  Inputs
        
        jobid - the id of the job (string or numeric)
 
 
  Description
 
        PApauseJob pauses the given Scheduler job. No new task will be scheduled until a call to PAresumeJob is made.
See also
PAkillJob, PAkillTask, PAresumeJob
