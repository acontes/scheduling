PAkillJob
  PAkillJob kills the given Scheduler job
 
  Syntax
 
        >> PAkillJob(jobid);
 
  Inputs
        
        jobid - the id of the job (string or numeric)
 
 
  Description
 
        PAkillJob kills the given Scheduler job.
